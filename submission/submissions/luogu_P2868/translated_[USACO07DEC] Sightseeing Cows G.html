[USACO07DEC] Sightseeing Cows G (翻译版)

题目描述

给你一张 nnn 点 mmm 边的有向图，第 iii 个点点权为 FiF_iFi​，第 iii 条边边权为 TiT_iTi​。

找一个环，设环上的点组成的集合为 SSS，环的边组成的集合为 EEE，最大化 ∑u∈SFu∑e∈ETe\dfrac{\sum_{u\in S}F_u}{\sum_{e\in E}T_e}∑e∈E​Te​∑u∈S​Fu​​。

数据范围：1≤n,Fi,Ti≤1031\leq n,F_i,T_i\leq 10^31≤n,Fi​,Ti​≤103，1≤m≤5×1031\leq m\leq 5\times10^31≤m≤5×103。

输入格式

* Line 1: Two space-separated integers: L and P

* Lines 2…L+1: Line i+1 contains a single one integer: Fi

* Lines L+2…L+P+1: Line L+i+1 describes cow path i with three space-separated integers: L1i , L2i , and Ti

输出格式

* Line 1: A single number given to two decimal places (do not perform explicit rounding), the maximum possible average fun per unit time, or 0 if the cows cannot plan any trip at all in accordance with the above rules.

时空限制

时间限制: 1000 ms
内存限制: 128 MB
