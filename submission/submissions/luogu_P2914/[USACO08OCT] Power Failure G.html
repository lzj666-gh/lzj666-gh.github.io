[USACO08OCT] Power Failure G

题目描述

A vicious thunderstorm has destroyed some of the wires of the farm’s electrical power grid! Farmer John has a map of all NNN (2≤N≤10002\le N \le 10002≤N≤1000) of the powerpoles, which are conveniently numbered 1…N1\ldots N1…N and located on integer plane coordinates (xi,yi)(x_i,y_i)(xi​,yi​) (−100000≤xi≤100000,−100000≤yi≤100000-100000 \le x_i \le 100000, -100000 \le y_i \le 100000−100000≤xi​≤100000,−100000≤yi​≤100000).

Some WWW (1≤W≤100001 \le W \le 100001≤W≤10000) power wires connect pairs of power poles PiP_iPi​ and PjP_jPj​ (1≤Pi≤N,1≤Pj≤N1 \le Pi \le N, 1 \le Pj \le N1≤Pi≤N,1≤Pj≤N).

He needs to get power from pole 111 to pole NNN (which means that some series of wires can traverse from pole 111 to pole NNN, probably through some intermediate set of poles).

Given the locations of the NNN poles and the list of remaining power wires, determine the minimum length of power wire required to restore the electrical connection so that electricity can flow from pole 111 to pole NNN. No wire can be longer than some real number MMM (0.0<M≤200000.00.0 < M \le 200000.00.0<M≤200000.0).

输入格式

Line 111: Two space-separated integers: NNN and WWW.

Line 222: A single real number: MMM.

Lines 3…N+23\ldots N+23…N+2: Each line contains two space-separated integers: xix_ixi​ and yiy_iyi​.

Lines N+3…N+2+WN+3\ldots N+2+WN+3…N+2+W: Two space-separated integers: PiP_iPi​ and PjP_jPj​.
